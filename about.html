About us

Our Company

Being a real estate company, Muwama walks with its shareholders in all aspects of life. At Muwama we cover some of the basic human needs i.e. Provide shelter and food. We start by providing clean, well sheltered properties. That is through buying and selling of land, plots, houses and house construction.

Secondly, we do interior designing, this is through glamming and house decoration. We have specialists who deal with different aspects in glamming of the house i.e. house painting, color coordination and curtain coordination which adds a lot of aesthetic appeal to the house. We also do landscaping and home kitchen-gardens in respect to our clientâ€™s preferential. Last but not least, we do provide food, which is through catering during events and providing agribusiness solutions to our clients. This ensures continuous supply of food. Direct from our farms we are able to supply food to individual homes and even during large gatherings. We do events planning be it weddings, birthdays, general meetings etc.

After all is said and done, we ensure our clients are able to maintain that lifestyle through having cleaning services, house management and maintenance of houses and homes. Our cleaning services include cleaning of floors, washing of clothes, vacuuming of carpets and house rugs. At muwama we do manage rental houses through rent collection, advertising vacant houses, ensuring proper sewerage system, zero water leakages and ensure security is provided through installation of CCTV camera and security alarms..
